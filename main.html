Having been established in 2011, The Melbourne Club of Stylophiles has served as a group for stylophiles to hang out and discuss their interests. Meetings are held at 5 Hotham Place, North Melbourne at the following times:

Season Meeting Time Frequency
Summer Fridays, 7pm-12am Every second week, starting from the first Friday of December
Autumn Saturdays, 6-11pm Every second week, starting from the first Saturday of March
Winter Sundays, 5-10pm Once a month, on the second Sunday of each month
Spring Saturdays, 6-11pm Every Saturday

ID is required to enter meetings as alcohol is served to members.

During each meeting, presentations by members are held. They are about any topic that is pen, pencil or writing related, perhaps a history of a brand or type of pen. Sometimes, they can also be a review of new pen releases or just a look at the architecture of a specific pen or pencil. Presentations can be about anything, just as it relates to the central focus of the club, pens, pencils and other writing implements.

If you are interested in presenting at the next MCS meeting, please contact us using this form or by talking to Willie Dustice (President), Todd Bonzalez (Vice President) or Mike Truk (Secretary) at a meeting.

MCS Membership

Membership costs are 20 dollars per month.

While it is not a requirement to be a member to attend meetings, non-members will not be able to present, vote in any elections, nor be served alcohol at meetings. Furthermore, they will not receive the quarterly packages sent to members that include pens, pencils and notebooks.

If you are interested in becoming a member, leave a message through our contact form or talk to Mike Truk (Secretary) at a meeting

Alternatively, you can email Mike Truk at jonothon11115@gmail.com.
